Moving a rectangle between non-overlapping (coordinate space) canvases

The canvas on the left and right are setup to represent non-overlapping coordinate space (e.g. different bases in Command & Conquer, Warhammer, Rise of Nations).

The canvas on the right has been translated by a canvas width (i.e. 400 pixels) to the right.

Use the 'wasd' keys to drive the rectangle between the two canvases and the 'r' key to reset.
